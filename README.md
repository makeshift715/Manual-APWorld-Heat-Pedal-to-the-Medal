# Manual-APWorld-Heat-Pedal-to-the-Medal

This is an APWorld for the board game Heat Pedal to the Medal. Race in 1960s racetracks. Play your cards right, manage your heat in your engine, and put your pedal to the metal.

Goal: Complete a single track in 1st place.
Setup: Complete normal setup instructions, except for the following:
	You start with 0 upgrade cards.
	Sort the upgrade cards and set them aside. Pay attention to the numbers in the bottom right corner of each card.
	Also set aside the extra heat and stress cards.
	Shuffle three extra Stress cards into your deck. You should have 6 total, before any weather.
	Remove three Heat cards from your engine. You should have 3 left over, before any weather.
	Shuffle and draw event cards until you get two different press corners, then put the press in those corners.
	Set up the Legends module. You should have at least 4 players and/or bots in the game, 6 if you're playing with the Rocky Road expansion.
	(Optional) Setup weather normally. Weather will not influence logic.
	In between rounds, you can choose to set up the game again or go again with the same board. You start with your choice of any number of upgrades, heat, or stress removals you collected.
	I recommend playing this on minimal accessibility, rather than full accessibility.
During gameplay: Follow the standard rules, except for the following:
	You start by only drawing up to 4 cards in step 9.
	There's an extra step 10 where you collect any items you received from Archipelago. Do not collect items after completing step 2.
	To collect items from Archipelago:
		Take a random Upgrade card from its corresponding pile and put it in your discard pile. Heat Upgrade cards go into your discard pile.
		Put any Heat cards (not from upgrades) into your engine.
		Remove any Stress cards from your hand or discard pile. If there are no Stress cards there, remove one from your deck and shuffle it.
	If you spin out, you do not send any checks that turn.
	When you shuffle your deck for any reason, you may first remove or add any upgrade cards you collected to your deck.
	You can quit a race and start a new one at any time.
Items: 
	Draw extra cards each turn.
	Upgrade cards.
	    17 Basic upgrade cards.
	    31 Advanced upgrade cards.
	    6 Upgrade cards for each expansion.
	Heat cards.
	Remove stress cards.
	Sponsorship cards. These will not be included in logic requirements and are intended to cheat logic.
	Traps:
		Heat trap. In step 10, pay a heat.
		Stress trap. During your next turn, you must boost for free.
Locations:
	Do cool stuff. The locations are sorted by when you should look out for them. Some examples include:
	    Play multiple cards with the same name in a turn.
	    Use a symbol on an upgrade card multiple times in a turn.
	    Many locations inspired by event cards.
